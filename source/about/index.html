--- layout: page title: "About me" date: 2013-04-05 16:35 comments: false sharing: true footer: true --- Graduated in CFD, I am now a research engineer at Inria. I acquired extensive experience with simulation codes and I could consider the lack of software engineering in their design. In particular, I noticed that code's modularity was most of the time neglected. With my collegues, we are involved in the design of a new paradigm for numerical simulation offering modularity, efficiency and visibility for researchers and engineers.
